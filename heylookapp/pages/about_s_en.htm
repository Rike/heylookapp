Hey Look wants to do things a little different.

We behind Hey Look are Michaela and Lotta, two women based in Helsinki with a background in event production and visual planning, photography and graphic design. After working in events for some time, our wish to concentrate on what we love most and do best grew so big that we decided to form Hey Look Oy in June 2011.

We love everything visual, are inspired by pretty details and big pictures and we want to share this passion with others. Our biggest reward is a beautiful end result and a happy client. We want to bring new ideas and solutions into the field and create spaces and decor with personality! Our aim is to decorate for one reaction: Hey Look!

We hope to hear from you soon!

Michaela & Lotta of Hey Look
