Services

Hey Look offers full service event design for private occasions. Our main aim is to make your event show your unique personality or create the theme of your choice to the last detail. Contact us and tell us about yourself and your special occasion. We’re happy to help you with ideas and planning right from the start. Or you already have a clear picture in mind, but cannot find the right decor pieces and materials anywhere? Contact us – we’ll find what your looking for or simply craft it ourselves.

In practice we take care of all design and planning, material sourcing, crafting, coordination and supplier selection and contacts. On your event day we’re there to set up, decorate, coordinate suppliers, make sure that every last detail is perfect, while you can concentrate on getting ready and preparing to meet your guests. After the event, we’ll take care of the take-down.

Our decor and design services include visual plans, mood boards, color scheme planning and floor mapping, decor production and styling, set-up and take-down, logistics and storage.

Graphic designs & prints

We also design and deliver quality graphic materials from invitations over menu cards to favor wrappings and thank you cards to match your event theme and personality.

Photography services

Hey Look also offers photography services that can be ordered separately or combined with event design services. We take pictures of every detail and every special moment to make sure you will remember your special day forever.

We love everything pretty and visual and want to share our creativity and experience with others. If you’re looking for event decor and design, let us help! We’re working closely with a range of suppliers in the field. Take a look at our partner companies for more information.

Our pricing is either hour- or project based depending on your needs. Event design and production as well as graphic design services are 45€ / h / service.
