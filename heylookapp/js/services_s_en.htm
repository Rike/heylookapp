services

To our corporate clients we offer creative services including, decor planning and production, graphic designs and photography. By outsourcing these services, you are guaranteed new, inspired ideas and cost effective and succesful results. Our intention is to create decors and designs that make you say: Hey Look!

Editorial content for decor-, lifestyle- and wedding magazines

We at Hey Look love to create inspiration! If you’re looking for content for your event- or wedding magazine, we’re happy to set up a beautiful story shoot, create ideas and instructions for related DIY projects and more. You’re a craft supply company looking for creative ways to feature your products or give your customers ideas on how to use your product range? Let us help! We’re crafty, creative and eager to help you put together marketing brochures, web content or whatever else you’re looking for.

The great news is that you won’t have to coordinate a range of stylists, photographers and prop suppliers – just leave the whole job to us and all you need to do is show the inspired results to your readers or customers.

Visual merchandise

If you’re looking for a new look for your sales space or need help to create an attractive window display, you’re at the right address! We take care that your visual merchandise brings out your products and services in the best way possible. Hey Look creates most decor pieces from scratch to make sure that your sales display won’t include the same items that three other shops down the street use, and that your merchandise will be truly unique.

We’re a young and creative team and we actively follow visual merchandise trends around the world. Like this we can always offer you new and fresh solutions for all your visual marketing needs.

Space decor and design

Hey Look wants to make spaces look great! If you’re in need for visual planning and decor for your restaurant, club, fair stand or any other space, we’re your partner! We love to create things from scratch and take care of your complete visual space planning and decor productions. You can lean back and wait for the impressive result. Or do you already have a clear picture in mind, but cannot find the right decor pieces anywhere? Contact us – we’ll find what your looking for or simply craft it ourselves.

Hey Look strives to create unique spaces that show personality. Our decor and design services include visual plans, mood boards, color scheme planning and floor mapping, decor production, set-up and take-down, logistics and storage.

Photography services
